Resin jhumka

Resin is a synthetic or natural polymer that can be molded into various shapes.Resin jhumkas can feature intricate designs, vibrant colors, and a glossy finish. These earrings can be worn for various occasions, from casual outings to festive celebrations. They complement both traditional Indian attire and contemporary outfits. Resin jhumkas are typically more affordable than gold or silver jhumkas, making them accessible for a broader audience.
